# WeTravel
An interactive Social Media App that allows users to review and post their travels with others on the platform. WeTravel aims to simplify traveling. No more lengthy blogs of an experience from one person. No more edited YouTube videos showcasing similar experiences. Enjoy an easy-to-use platform for travel.

With WeTravel, a community of travelers and soon-to-be travelers can upload their own unique story. Showcase your experiences more freely and increase your chances of being noticed. You are not limited by your level of editing or writing, for WeTravel inherits a more familiar interface. Your experience is very important. Come and show your traveling experience!
